## Description

Exercise Cyber Star is a Singaporean nation-wide cybersecurity exercise which in 2019, gathered more than 250 participants from the public and private sectors. It is organized by the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA) as a government effort to test Singaporeâ€™s cyber-incident management and emergency response plans and enhance communication across sectors. The exercise involved all eleven critical information infrastructures - government, infocomm, energy, aviation, maritime, land transport, healthcare, banking, finance, water, security, emergency, and media. Among others, scenarios included Internet connectivity disruptions, communication network disruptions, domain name system manipulation, and phishing.
